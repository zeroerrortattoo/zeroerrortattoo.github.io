Welcome to Zero Error Tattoos.

In our tattoo studio we never reuse ink or needles. For every client and every tattoo we use a new sterile needle which is immediately discarded in a bio hazard-safe container after our work is finished.

ABOUT

Zero Error Tattoos

Zero Error Tattoos (ZET) was established on 12 Feb. 2016 by an Electrical Engineer who wanted to pursue his passion of tattooing.



We have state of the art tattoo studio in SFS Colony, Mansarovar, Jaipur.

We at Zero Error Tattoo studio specialize in freestyle and customized tattoos. You can always walk in with any concept and we would confidently shape it into reality.
